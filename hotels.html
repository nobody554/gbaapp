Hotel Partners


GBA has partnered with 5 local hotels to offer alumni and families an opportunity to book discounted hotel rooms.


GBA has blocked a limited number of discounted rooms at Radisson, Staybridge Suites, Embassy Suites, Holiday Inn - Civic Center, and Best Western - Lubbock Windsor. These rooms will be reserved on a first come basis and you will be responsible for all reservations/cancellations/hotel requirements, etc. GBA has only secured the reduced rate - each individual party will be responsible for their own arrangements/payments, etc.


Please review the information on the GBA website for additional details and requirements for each hotel. Remember, ABD is on a holiday weekend so rates have been increased and hotels will fill up quickly. There are only a limited number of reserved rooms at each hotel below so reserve before the first week in August.


Breakfast: The Radisson Hotel is giving a special breakfast buffet discount of $8.95 per person, just mention you are with the Tech Goin' Band. You do not have to stay at the Radisson to receive the discounted breakfast.


The Radisson Hotel located at 5th & Avenue Q is offering a discounted rate of $75/night. Reservations must be made by 8/10 by calling 806-747-0171 and asking for the Goin’ Band Association rate. Radisson is also giving GBA members the special $75/night rate for stays throughout the next year (special event weekends are excluded - Home football games, graduations, etc.)
